What We Do

The Lonah Maiko Foundation is dedicated to creating lasting change in communities through integrated development programs across education, health, and water, sanitation, and hygiene (WASH).

Education Programs
Our Education Programs focus on giving every learner an equal chance to succeed by removing the barriers that hold children back. Through direct support to schools, mentorship initiatives, learning materials, and community engagement, we work to create nurturing environments where students can thrive academically and socially. By partnering with teachers, parents, and local leaders, we ensure that learners not only access education but also receive the guidance, encouragement, and resources they need to unlock their full potential. Our goal is to build confident, empowered students who can shape a brighter future for themselves and their communities.
Healthcare Initiatives
Community health initiatives in rural and hard-to-reach areas are designed to address gaps caused by distance, limited infrastructure, health-worker shortages, and poverty. These initiatives focus on bringing healthcare closer to communities, improving disease prevention, and empowering local populations to take charge of their own health. This involves a wide range of activities such as health education, mobile clinics, CHW training, nutrition programs, WASH improvements, and disease-specific interventions. These programs address challenges like limited access to healthcare, high disease burden, and poverty-driven health disparities.
WASH Programs
Our WASH Programs strengthen community health and dignity by improving access to clean water, sanitation, and proper hygiene practices. We collaborate with communities, institutions, and health facilities to implement sustainable solutions such as safe water systems, sanitation facilities, and hygiene awareness campaigns. These efforts help reduce disease, promote healthier living, and create safer learning and home environments. Through continuous education, community involvement, and long-term support, we work to ensure that families and children enjoy the fundamental right to clean water and a hygienic environment, leading to healthier and stronger communities.
Student Sponsorship
Our Student Sponsorship Program is designed to support vulnerable learners who face financial and social barriers that threaten their education. By providing school fees, uniforms, learning materials, and mentorship, we help students remain in school and stay focused on their academic goals. Each sponsorship is a lifeline that restores hope, stability, and opportunity for children who would otherwise be left behind. Through close collaboration with schools, families, and community leaders, we ensure that every sponsored learner receives holistic support—empowering them to pursue their dreams and build a stronger future for themselves and their communities.
Community Empowerment
Our Community Empowerment Program strengthens families and local groups by equipping them with the knowledge, tools, and opportunities they need to improve their own livelihoods. Through training, capacity building, awareness campaigns, and collaborative projects, we help communities develop practical solutions to the challenges they face in education, health, and daily living. By encouraging participation, leadership, and shared responsibility, we create an environment where people can take charge of their growth and well-being. This approach ensures long-lasting impact, builds resilience, and fosters a united community committed to creating a better future for all.
Strategic Partnerships
Our Strategic Partnerships Program focuses on building strong, meaningful collaborations that expand our impact and strengthen community development. By working closely with schools, health facilities, government agencies, NGOs, and private sector partners, we align shared goals and pool resources to address critical needs in Education, Health, and WASH. These partnerships allow us to implement larger, more sustainable initiatives that reach more families and deliver long-term results. Through transparency, shared responsibility, and mutual commitment, we create powerful alliances that drive positive change, enhance service delivery, and support the growth of resilient, empowered communities.

Our Mission and Vision

Our Mission

To improve the quality of life for disadvantaged communities in Africa through integrated development programs that prioritize education, health, and environmental sustainability.

Our Vision

A future where every child has access to quality education, every community has access to healthcare and clean water, and sustainable development creates opportunity for all.

The Lonah Maiko Foundation

The Lonah Maiko Foundation (LMF) is a community-centered organisation dedicated to improving the quality of life for children, families, and underserved communities in Kenya. Founded on the belief that every person deserves dignity, opportunity, and a healthy environment, LMF works to bridge gaps in Education, Health, and WASH (Water, Sanitation & Hygiene). We collaborate closely with schools, health facilities, local leaders, and partners to bring practical, sustainable solutions that directly transform lives. From supporting learners, promoting healthy communities, to advancing clean water and sanitation, our approach is rooted in compassion, transparency, and long-term impact. Guided by strong values and a passion for service, LMF continues to provide hope, empower communities, and create pathways for a brighter, more resilient future.
