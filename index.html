What We Do

The Lonah Maiko Foundation is dedicated to creating lasting change in communities through integrated development programs across education, health, and water, sanitation, and hygiene (WASH).

Education Programs
Providing scholarships and support for students, with special focus on young women empowerment and education access.
Healthcare Initiatives
Community health initiatives in rural and hard-to-reach areas are designed to address gaps caused by distance, limited infrastructure, health-worker shortages, and poverty. These initiatives focus on bringing healthcare closer to communities, improving disease prevention, and empowering local populations to take charge of their own health. This involves a wide range of activities such as health education, mobile clinics, CHW training, nutrition programs, WASH improvements, and disease-specific interventions. These programs address challenges like limited access to healthcare, high disease burden, and poverty-driven health disparities.
WASH Programs
Building water infrastructure, improving sanitation facilities, and promoting hygiene practices in underserved communities.
Student Sponsorship
Connecting generous donors with deserving students to provide educational opportunities and mentorship.
Community Empowerment
Strengthening communities through skill development, capacity building, and sustainable livelihood programs.
Strategic Partnerships
Collaborating with local and international organizations to amplify impact and scale sustainable solutions.

Our Mission and Vision

Our Mission

To improve the quality of life for disadvantaged communities in Africa through integrated development programs that prioritize education, health, and environmental sustainability.

Our Vision

A future where every child has access to quality education, every community has access to healthcare and clean water, and sustainable development creates opportunity for all.

How We Started

The Journey of The Lonah Maiko Foundation

The Lonah Maiko Foundation was established with a vision to transform lives and communities in Africa. Founded on principles of compassion, sustainability, and community empowerment, we began our journey by addressing critical gaps in education, health, and water access in underserved communities.

Since our inception, we have remained committed to creating meaningful, lasting impact through integrated development programs that empower individuals and strengthen communities.
