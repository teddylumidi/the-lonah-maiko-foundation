Our Health Programs

Good health is the foundation for development. We provide comprehensive healthcare services and health education to vulnerable communities.

Preventive Care
Health screening programs, vaccination campaigns, and disease prevention initiatives to keep communities healthy.
Maternal & Child Health
Prenatal care, skilled birth attendance, and postnatal support to ensure healthy pregnancies and childbirth.
Health Education
Training communities on nutrition, hygiene, disease prevention, and healthy lifestyle practices.
Mental Health Support
Counseling, psychological support, and mental health awareness programs for holistic wellbeing.
Healthcare Access
Mobile clinics, subsidized healthcare services, and support for essential medicines in remote areas.
Community Health Workers
Training and equipping community health workers to provide first-line healthcare and health education.

Health Targets

Reduce Maternal Mortality

Working to reduce maternal deaths through improved healthcare access and quality prenatal and postnatal care.

Improve Child Nutrition

Ensuring children receive adequate nutrition through school feeding programs and nutrition education.

Increase Health Literacy

Empowering communities with knowledge to make informed health decisions and seek appropriate care.
