The Lonah Maiko Foundation relies on the generosity of supporters who believe in creating lasting change for children, families, and underserved communities in Kenya. Every contribution directly strengthens our work in Education, Health, and WASH, enabling us to provide school support, promote healthy living, and expand access to clean water and sanitation. Your donation helps us deliver practical, community-driven solutions that restore dignity, offer opportunity, and improve daily life for those who need it most.

We are committed to transparency, accountability, and long-term impact. By giving to LMF, you become part of a compassionate mission dedicated to empowering communities and building a brighter, more resilient future. Whether you choose to support a student, strengthen our WASH projects, or contribute to our broader community programs, your partnership makes a real and immediate difference.

100% Donation Guarantee

At The Lonah Maiko Foundation, every cent donated by partner schools and individual student sponsors goes directly to Africa to support the exact person or community for whom it was intended. We ensure that 100% of donor contributions reach the ground—funding education support, WASH initiatives, and community programs without any deductions. Your giving directly transforms lives, strengthens families, and uplifts vulnerable learners and communities with full transparency and accountability.
