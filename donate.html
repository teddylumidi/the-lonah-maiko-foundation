Where Your Donation Goes

Education (40%)

Student scholarships, teacher training, school infrastructure, and educational materials to ensure quality education for all.

Health (30%)

Healthcare services, health education, maternal health programs, and medical supplies for vulnerable communities.

WASH (20%)

Water systems, sanitation facilities, and hygiene education programs to ensure safe water and sanitation.

Administration (10%)

Essential operational costs to ensure effective program management and transparency in fund utilization.

Tax & Donation Information

Tax-Deductible Donations

The Lonah Maiko Foundation is a registered non-profit organization. Your donation may be tax-deductible. Please consult your tax advisor or visit our organization page for details.

Secure Donations

All donations are processed securely through our trusted payment partners. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your financial information.

Transparency & Impact

We are committed to transparency. Receive regular updates on how your donation is being used and the impact you're making in communities.
