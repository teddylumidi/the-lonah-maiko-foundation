Corporate Social Responsibility

Partner with The Lonah Maiko Foundation to fulfill your corporate social responsibility while creating meaningful impact in communities.

Partnership Opportunities

Financial Sponsorship
Corporate grants and funding for specific programs or projects. Benefit from corporate tax deductions and recognition.
Skills & Expertise
Provide professional expertise in areas like technology, business training, healthcare, and infrastructure.
Employee Engagement
Organize employee volunteer days, team building through community projects, and matching gift programs.
Product Donations
Donate products or services such as technology, educational materials, or professional services to our programs.
Joint Fundraising
Co-host fundraising events to support our programs while raising your company's profile.
Impact Reporting
Receive detailed reports on the impact of your partnership and the difference you're making in communities.

Why Partner With Us

Proven Track Record

We have a proven track record of implementing effective programs with measurable impact in education, health, and WASH sectors.

Transparency & Accountability

Full transparency in fund utilization, regular reporting, and third-party evaluations ensure your investment creates real impact.

Tax Benefits

As a registered non-profit, corporate donations are typically tax-deductible. Consult your accountant for details.

Brand Enhancement

Partner with a trusted foundation and showcase your company's commitment to social responsibility and sustainable development.
