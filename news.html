Faith's Journey: From Poverty to Promise

Meet Faith, a bright young girl who was on the brink of child labor in tea plantations. Thanks to a Lonah Maiko Foundation scholarship, Faith now attends school daily, receives new uniforms, stationery, and even daily lunch through our Scholarship Plus program. Her story exemplifies how education transforms lives.

Education vs. Exploitation: Saving Futures from Child Labor

Many children in our program areas face the harsh choice between education and child labor in tea plantations, quarries, or worse. Our scholarships provide the alternative they deserve—quality education, uniforms, books, and hope for a brighter future free from exploitation.

Scholarship Program Updates: Rising to Meet Needs

Due to rising living costs in Kenya, we've updated our scholarship fees for the first time in 5 years. Basic scholarships are now $390/year, while Scholarship Plus (including daily school lunch) is $660/year. These funds ensure comprehensive support for our students' education journey.
