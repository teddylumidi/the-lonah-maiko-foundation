About The Lonah Maiko Foundation

Transforming lives through education, health, and sustainable development

Who We Are

The Lonah Maiko Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving the quality of life for disadvantaged communities in Africa. We focus on three critical areas: education, health, and water, sanitation, and hygiene (WASH).

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To improve the quality of life for disadvantaged communities in Africa through integrated development programs that prioritize education, health, and environmental sustainability.

Our Vision

A future where every child has access to quality education, every community has access to healthcare and clean water, and sustainable development creates opportunity for all.

Our Core Values

Compassion
We genuinely care about the wellbeing of the communities we serve and approach our work with empathy and respect.
Integrity
We operate with transparency, accountability, and ethical practices in all our programs and partnerships.
Sustainability
We design programs that create lasting impact and empower communities to sustain progress over the long term.
Collaboration
We work in partnership with communities, organizations, and stakeholders to achieve greater impact together.
